Quick Eco Tips
✅
Rinse Before Recycling
Always rinse containers to remove food residue before placing them in the recycle bin. Contaminated recyclables can ruin an entire batch.
🌱
Compost Food Scraps
Fruit peels, vegetable scraps, coffee grounds, and eggshells all belong in the bio bin — they create nutrient-rich compost for plants.
🚫
Pizza Boxes ≠ Recyclable
Greasy pizza boxes contaminate cardboard recycling. If the top is clean, recycle it; the greasy bottom goes in the non-bio bin.
🔋
Batteries Need Special Care
Never throw batteries in the trash! They contain toxic chemicals. Most hardware stores have special battery recycling drop-off points.
♻️
Check the Recycling Symbol
Not all plastics are equal. Look for the number inside the recycling triangle — #1 and #2 are most widely accepted at recycling centers.
